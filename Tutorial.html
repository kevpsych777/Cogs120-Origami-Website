Dragon!!!

Name: Pikachu Difficulty: 3/5
Creator: AshKetchup27 Estimated Time: 20min
User Rating: 4.1/5 Paper Requirement: 7in x 7in
Bookmark Create This Now!

STEP ONE

Start with a square piece of origami paper, color side down.

STEP TWO

Fold the left half to the right, then unfold.

STEP THREE

Fold the bottom half to the top.

STEP FOUR

Fold both layers of the top triangle corner to the bottom edge. Make sure the tip of the corner lines up with the vertical crease in the center.

STEP FIVE

Fold the left and right sides up diagonally towards the center.

STEP SIX

Fold the left and right corners to the center.

STEP SEVEN

Fold the left ear diagonally to the side.

STEP EIGHT

Fold the right ear to the side.

STEP NINE

Fold the bottom corner up to flatten the origami pikachu's chin.

STEP TEN

Fold in the pointy cheeks.
